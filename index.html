    • Games

    Space Invaders

    A fixed shooter in which the player controls a space ship by moving it horizontally across the bottom of the screen and firing at descending aliens. Destroy as much aliens as you can and don't let them touch you!

    Driving Simulator

    Drive. Collect Coins. Crash. Repeat. Do you ever just want to drive around in an open world game where you can do anything you want? Collect coins to purchase different paint jobs to customize your car in Driving Simulator!

    The Eternal Fight

    An open world FPS (first person shooter) game where you fight evil robots and collect powerups to become even stronger. Store items in you inventory and use them later for your advantage. Fight till you can fight anymore!

    Bacteria Be-Gone

    Has Coronavirus gotten on your nerves and you need to blow off some steam. Come destroy Coronavirus with this 2D shooter where you can fight off COVID-19 particles and purchase items to make it even easier!

    Endless Runner

    An endless runner game with a twist. You assume the role of an animal in which they all have their own abilities and you have to run away from your owner that wants to give you a bath. Dont get caught!
